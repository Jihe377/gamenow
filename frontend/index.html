Chess

1. Host creates the room, guest joins with the room code.

2. Both players click Ready — colors are randomly assigned.

3. White moves first. Click a piece to select, then click the destination.

4. Checkmate, stalemate, or draw by repetition/insufficient material ends the game automatically.

5. You may offer a draw or resign at any time.

Gomoku

1. Both players join the room and click Ready — colors are randomly assigned.

2. Black moves first. Click any empty intersection to place your stone.

3. First to connect five stones in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.

4. If the board fills up with no winner, the game is a draw.

Battleship

1. Place your full fleet on your grid before the match starts.

2. Ships cannot touch: any two ships must be separated by at least one cell in all eight directions (including diagonals).

3. Each round, both players fire once; the round resolves after both shots.

4. Hits and misses are revealed when the round ends. Sink every enemy ship to win.
